## Design Style

Use a dark, futuristic SaaS aesthetic.

- Deep purple-to-black gradient backgrounds
- Minimal, clean, and premium layout
- Bold, modern, high-contrast typography
- Generous spacing and strong visual hierarchy
- Subtle depth using layered sections (no heavy effects)
- Soft glow accents to suggest a polished, AI-driven feel

Overall tone should feel:
- Tech-forward
- Sleek and modern
- Premium and investor-ready
- Inspired by brands like Stripe, Linear, and modern AI startups

Avoid:
- Cluttered layouts
- Bright or playful color palettes
- Overly complex visuals or heavy effects
